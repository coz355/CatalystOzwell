Lifestyle, Health & Safety
Context-aware guidance • Location sensitive
GROKV35
CLOTHING & SUN PROTECTION
Recommended Layers
Breathable base layer + light outer shell. UV-protective clothing strongly advised when UV > 6.
Sun Cream Guidance
Broad spectrum SPF 30+ (minimum). Reapply every 2 hours. Higher SPF (50+) recommended for fair skin or high UV locations (Turkey, Middle East, Southern Europe in summer).
EATING, DRINKING & HYDRATION
Daily Hydration Target
2.5–3.5 liters of water per day in hot climates. Increase by 500ml for every hour of outdoor activity. Avoid excessive caffeine/alcohol.
Eating Recommendations
Light, frequent meals. Prioritize local seasonal produce. In high heat: avoid heavy proteins after 2pm. Carry high-electrolyte snacks when hiking.
HEALTH GUIDANCE
GENERAL WELLBEING
Maintain consistent sleep. Walk 8,000+ steps daily when possible. Monitor air quality (AQI) before outdoor activity.
HEAT & COLD STRESS
Recognize early signs of heat exhaustion. In cold climates: layer and stay dry. Never ignore shivering or dizziness.
MEDICAL NOTE
This is general guidance only. Consult a local physician for personal medical advice. Carry any required medication.
